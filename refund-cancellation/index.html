Policies

Refund and cancellation policy

Clear rules help avoid confusion around subscriptions, setup fees, custom builds and domains.

Website subscriptions

You can cancel future subscription billing, but past periods already provided are generally not refunded unless required by law or agreed in writing.

Assisted setup

Assisted setup is a one-off support service. Once work has started, refunds may be limited to the unused portion, at PBI’s discretion.

Custom build deposits

Custom build deposits reserve work time and may be non-refundable once planning or build work has started.

Domains

Domain registrations and renewals may be non-refundable once processed by the domain provider.

SEO Care

SEO Care is billed as an ongoing service and does not guarantee rankings, traffic, leads or revenue.
